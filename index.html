About Us

When trappers, explorers and guides in the 1800s pushed west they entered unique and unknown territories filled new vistas, rivers, mountains, hills and terrains that were not on any map. These adventurers had to quickly orient themselves to keep from being lost. One key to their success was looking far into the horizons and finding a “Far Point” or prominent feature to focus on. That Far Point was the focal point, the place to go, until these men needed another Far Point as they continued their journey. As they wandered into hidden valleys, deep forests, sharp river bends and unknown lands these adventurers could always re-find that Far Point and adjust their paths as the need arose.

Far Point Colorado works with businesses and entrepreneurs to establish their own Far Point and one of the first places to start is the legal organization/format that will be used as the business forges into the future. The next phase is making those plans work.
